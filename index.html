About Brivent

Structure for
Ambition.

We are a parent company that builds products, nurtures platforms, and supports early-stage ventures — the scaffolding that holds big ideas upright while they grow.

WhatsApp Sales & Ordering System
Live
Product Overview

We help businesses turn WhatsApp into a smart sales system. Customers can chat, get recommendations, and place orders automatically — while the business receives everything in a clean, organized way.

What It Does

  • Auto-replies to customer messages instantly
  • Recommends products and meals based on customer interest
  • Handles orders from start to finish, seamlessly
  • Reduces missed messages and lost sales opportunities
  • Improves overall customer experience and satisfaction

Who It's For

Restaurants, vendors, and businesses that receive orders on WhatsApp and want to convert more conversations into sales with less manual effort.

BIIT — Be Involved In Tech
Beta
Community Initiative

BIIT is a community-driven initiative helping young people step into technology through practical learning, mentorship, and real-world exposure. We create opportunities for students and beginners to discover tech, build skills, and connect with a growing ecosystem of builders.

What We Offer

  • Practical, hands-on learning experiences in tech
  • Mentorship from real builders and practitioners
  • Real-world exposure and project opportunities
  • A growing network of motivated builders and peers
  • Access to the broader Brivent ecosystem of products

Who It's For

Students, beginners, and young people who want to discover technology, build real skills, and grow through an active and supportive community.
